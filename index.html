Who am I?

I'm a passionate UI/UX designer and mobile developer with a unique blend of creative and technical skills. I specialize in creating user-centered designs and implementing them as functional, beautiful applications.

With expertise in both design tools like Figma and development frameworks like React Native and Flutter, I can take a project from concept to completion while ensuring the final product stays true to the original vision.
